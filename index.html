Seashore riddled with plastic

Seashores, once pristine, are now littered with plastic, harming marine life. Bottles and microplastics disrupt biodiversity and scar landscapes. Tackling this demands reducing plastic use and better waste management..

Mountains made of plastic

Mountains made entirely of plastic waste stand as stark symbols of humanity's pollution crisis. These towering piles of discarded bottles, bags, and debris threaten ecosystems and leach harmful chemicals into the environment.

City streets filled with loads of plastic

City streets are increasingly filled with plastic waste, things such as bottles, bags, and wrappers scatter the sidewalks, creating an eyesore and blocking drains. This growing problem harms the environment, contributes to pollution, and impacts urban life.
